Privacy Policy

Sawyer & Otto Adventures — Activities with Ryan
Last updated: July 3, 2026

What we collect

This game collects the minimum needed to work:

Leaderboard entries. If you submit a score, we store the display name you type, your score, level reached, and run time on our server (hosted on Vercel with Upstash Redis). Names are filtered for profanity. Please don't enter your real name or personal information as a display name.

Local game progress. Your level progress, best scores, best times, and star medals are saved in your browser's local storage on your own device. This data never leaves your device and you can clear it anytime through your browser settings.

What we don't collect

No accounts, no emails, no passwords, no location data, no advertising identifiers, and no tracking cookies. We do not sell or share any data with third parties.

Third-party platforms

When the game is played on a portal site (such as CrazyGames or Poki), that platform may show advertisements and collect its own data under its own privacy policy. We do not control or receive that data. Please review the hosting platform's privacy policy for details.

Children

The game is suitable for general audiences. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. The only user-entered data is an optional leaderboard display name, which is profanity-filtered and should not contain personal information.

Data removal

To remove a leaderboard entry, contact us with the display name and approximate submission date. To remove local progress, clear your browser's site data for this game.

Contact

Questions about this policy: contact Activities with Ryan via the publishing platform where you found this game.
