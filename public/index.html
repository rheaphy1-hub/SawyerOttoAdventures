FROZEN PEAK

Climb the icy slopes with Otto as avalanches rumble and frost creatures emerge from the snow.

Grab glowing ice crystals (75 pts), defeat yetis & frozen foes (100 pts), defeat the Blizzard King (+750)!

SANDY SHORES

Otto spotted a hermit crab and now they're sprinting down the beach. Dodge the surf, brave the pier, and defeat King Crab Krawley!

Collect seashells (75 pts), pearl shells (150 pts), starfish (250 pts), defeat crabs & gulls (100–150 pts), beat the boss (+1 000)!

UNDER THE SEA

Plunge into a bioluminescent alien ocean. Dodge glowing tentacles, leap over pressure pods, and surface to face Leviathan Lyra!

Collect pearls (75–250 pts), stomp glowfish & cephalopods (100–150 pts), beat the boss (+1 000)!

SPOOKY HAUS

You've entered a haunted German manor. Creaking floors, dancing candlelight, and spectral guardians await — survive to meet Baron Von Scream!

Grab coins & spell orbs (75–250 pts), defeat ghosts, zombies & gargoyles (100–150 pts), beat the boss (+1 000)!

OUTER SPACE

The final frontier. Drift through a derelict station amid drones, turrets, and radiation — then duel the AI overlord, Nexus Nine!

Collect cells (75–250 pts), defeat drones & robots (100–150 pts), beat the final boss (+1 000)!
