Sawyer & Otto: Epic Adventures 🌵

Join Sawyer and his baby brother Otto across desert dunes, icy peaks, and mysterious realms — explore, collect, and overcome epic challenges!

Score: 0  |  ❤️ 3 You are Sawyer: ←→ move   ↑ jump   Space throw ball
Otto, your buddy: the baby follows you on his own, hops along behind, and spits milk at any critter that gets close. He can't be hurt — only Sawyer loses hearts.
How to play: Each adventure features unique challenges. Grab collectibles for points, bonk or stomp enemies with soccer balls or by jumping on them. Complete level objectives to advance to the next world. Epic bosses guard the end of each adventure — dodge their attacks and hit them multiple times to claim victory!
Power-ups: Baby Bottles 🍼 grant temporary shields (protect you from one hit) and boost Otto's milk-spitting power for extra damage against enemies and bosses. Look for them hidden throughout each level!
